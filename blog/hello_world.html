Hello World / Hola Mundo

Over the years after many conversations with friends about niche topics, staying up late at night pondering life on the verge of an existential crisis, we have arrived here… a blog in 2018? Bueno, mejor que nada… This post is titled 'Hello World / Hola Mundo', it symbolizes the first step we take to attempt to communicate to someone or something else while simultaneously being born into a new digital world of our creation.

I want you to step into my world and I'll take you with me on this journey of pursuing my master's degree in Computer Science. To me I see Computer Science as attaining a new ability to be able to see the world through a computational lens, the opportunity to creatively solve problems and to contribute, create and share knowledge with others. As someone who in the past has been deeply involved with community-building as a former community organizer, teacher and mentor – I seek to bridge technology and social justice. I expect this journey to be filled with moments of joy, tears, and frustration… Pa'lante!
