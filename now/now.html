What I'm doing now

Hello World

Over the years after many conversations with friends about niche topics; staying up late at night pondering life and on the verge of an existential crisis, here we are… A blog in 2018? Bueno, mejor que nada.. better than starting in 2019…

I want to take you with me on my journey of pursuing my master's degree in Computer Science. I expect this journey to be filled with twists and turns, moments of joy, tears, and frustration.

Why Computer Science? Why now? To me I see Computer Science as gaining a new ability, to be able see the world through a computational lens, to have the opportunity to contribute, create and share knowledge with others.

The direction of this blog is to post about what I'm learning in the classes that I'm taking. While at the same time I intend to teach others what I'm learning about in an accessible way.

In addition a location for different projects and or interests.

A place to share different ideas about the intersection for technology and social justice.
